about me.

Hi! My name is Tara Brooks, I am a UX/UI and graphic designer in Portland, Oregon.

I’ve been called a process guru because my super power is developing new processes that work. By day I enjoy solving and communicating complicated problems through graphic design and user interface solutions. In my free-time, I enjoy digging for the best vintage finds at local flea markets, daydreaming about tattoos and traveling, and throwing myself into new hobbies like renovating vintage trailers or silversmithing. I’ve never been afraid to get my hands dirty and learn something new.

I am a passionate problem solver, nothing motivates me more than sparking an efficient and thoughtful solution. I carry that drive throughout my work as a UX/UI designer, through clean, fresh and contemporary concepts that create an ideal experience for any user from the second they interact.

My UX Skills

User Research

My user research approach is to find the problem with a product through user interviews. Through interviews, I understand how a real person interacts with the product and then focus on crafting solutions based around real-world feedback.

User Empathy

Through user interviews and analysis, I develop an understanding of how a user creates a relationship with a product. By talking through this relationship, I find pain points and anticipate any stressful scenarios associated with using the product. From there, I create a seamless experience for the user.

User Interface Design

I enjoy designing and redesigning to create the ideal experience for a user. I seek to discover what makes a user tick. I carry by graphic design expertise combined with user testing and interviews to create a product that will provide a solution to user problems.
